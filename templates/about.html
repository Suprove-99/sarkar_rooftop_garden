Sarkar's Rooftop Garden

They love to experiment and interestingly they have been successful to have fruits like "sugarcane plant", "Watermelon", "Pineapple" etc which are not so popular to have in your rooftop garden. This gardening is a good practice for both mental and physical health for the family.
